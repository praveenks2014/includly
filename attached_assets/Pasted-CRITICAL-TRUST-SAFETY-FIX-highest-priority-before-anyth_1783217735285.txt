CRITICAL TRUST & SAFETY FIX — highest priority, before anything else.
The audit confirmed there is NO verification gate: any specialist,
including a therapist with zero credentials, can become active, appear
in parent search, and get matched with a child. This is a child-safety
hole and, for therapists, a legal violation (RCI Act requires
registration to practice). This MUST be fixed before launch.

The infrastructure exists but isn't enforced. Wire it up:

1. LISTABILITY GATE (server-side, non-negotiable): a specialist must NOT
   appear in ANY parent-facing search/match result, and must NOT be
   assignable to a child, unless BOTH:
   a. adminApproved = true (an admin has reviewed and approved them), AND
   b. their vertical's required verification is complete (see #2).
   Enforce this in the SEARCH/MATCH query itself, server-side — not the
   UI. paymentActivated alone must NOT make someone searchable.

2. VERTICAL-SPECIFIC REQUIRED FIELDS (block "profile complete /
   submittable for review" until met):
   - THERAPIST: RCI/CRR number MANDATORY + RCI certificate document
     upload MANDATORY. Cannot submit for approval without both.
   - ALL VERTICALS: at least one government ID document upload MANDATORY.
   - SHADOW TEACHER: ID mandatory; training certificate strongly
     encouraged (allow submit without it, but flag to admin / lower
     trust). Confirm this is the right line.
   - Make these server-side validations, not just UI required-attributes.

3. VERIFICATION WORKFLOW: after a specialist submits, they enter
   "pending review" (NOT active/searchable). An admin reviews documents
   in the existing admin UI, then approves (sets adminApproved=true) or
   rejects with reason. Only approval makes them eligible to appear to
   parents (combined with #1).

4. DECOUPLE payment-activation from verification: completing onboarding /
   paying any fee must NOT auto-set searchability. Searchability =
   adminApproved AND required-verification-complete, enforced in the
   query.

5. BACKFILL: any existing specialists currently searchable without
   verification — set them to pending review / not-searchable until an
   admin approves. Don't grandfather unverified specialists into
   visibility.

server-side required-field validation per vertical, and how "pending
review → approved → searchable" flows. This is the launch-blocking
safety fix — verify the gate is SERVER-SIDE, and test that an
unverified/unapproved therapist genuinely cannot appear to a parent.
