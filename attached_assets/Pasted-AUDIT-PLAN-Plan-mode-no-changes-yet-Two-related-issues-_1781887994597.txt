ISSUE 1 — Buyout ends engagement immediately instead of on effectiveEndDate:
The buyout flow currently flips the engagement to "ended" immediately
when payment is confirmed (or when admin approves). This is WRONG.
A 15-day buyout means the engagement continues for 15 more days and
ends automatically on effectiveEndDate. The payment is a fee for
shorter notice, not an immediate stop.

Correct behaviour:
- Parent pays buyout fee → engagement stays ACTIVE (not ended)
- Both parent and teacher see a clear banner: "Engagement ending on
  [effectiveEndDate]" — countdown or date prominently shown
- On effectiveEndDate, engagement automatically transitions to "ended"
- Teacher continues working (and receiving salary) until effectiveEndDate
- No admin approval needed for the standard flow (per #81)
