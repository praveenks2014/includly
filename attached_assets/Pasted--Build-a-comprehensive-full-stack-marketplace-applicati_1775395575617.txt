Business & Compliance Strategy
To make this app successful and compliant with App Store/Google Play rules, keep these points in mind:
1. Global vs. Local Identity (Aadhar)
Aadhar is unique to India. For a global app, you should use a KYC (Know Your Customer) provider like Stripe Identity or Sumsub. This allows you to verify Passports, DLs, or National IDs worldwide while using Aadhar specifically for the Indian market.
2. Data Privacy (HIPAA & GDPR)
Because you are listing Neurologists and Pediatricians, you may inadvertently handle sensitive health data (PHI).
* GDPR (Europe): You must allow users to delete their data.
* HIPAA (USA): If you store medical records or session notes, your database must be HIPAA-compliant (Supabase and AWS offer this).
* DPDP (India): Ensure you have explicit consent checkboxes for Aadhar and data processing.
3. Monetization Nuance
The "NoBroker" model works because it saves users high brokerage fees.
* Parent Side: Charge for "Verified Contact Unlocks."
* Professional Side: Keep it free to list initially to build your database, then offer "Featured Listing" spots for a fee.
